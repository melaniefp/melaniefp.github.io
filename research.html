I want my research to have an impact in the world, for which I collaborate regularly with psychiatrists at Massachusetts General Hospital, and experts in Human Computer Interaction.

Some of the general questions I am trying to answer: How can we better quantify uncertainty (when should you trust your model)? How can we design expressive, interpretable priors in Bayesian models? How can we combine human knowledge with data-driven evidence?
